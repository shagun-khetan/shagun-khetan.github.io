I am an undergraduate studying Economics, Math and Finance at Ashoka University, where I work on empirical questions related to labor markets, public finance, financial markets, and institutional systems using large administrative and survey datasets. My recent work has involved constructing longitudinal higher-education datasets from fragmented government sources, analyzing NSS and PLFS microdata, and working with firm-level MCA financial filings to study lending and market outcomes. I am broadly interested in applied econometrics, macro-finance, political economy, and empirical research on markets and institutions, and have recently been exploring asset pricing and systematic investing through quantitative market analysis.

Work aside, I enjoy reading and writing across disciplines, listening to and playing music, sketching and learning new things.
